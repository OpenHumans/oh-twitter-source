Twitter is a social media website to which you can post tweets and like/share other tweets. Depending on how you use the site, this may include: Your own tweets, retweets you have made, tweets you have liked, images/videos you have shared, your location if you geo-tag your tweets. This website can be used to retrieve and store detailed information about your tweets and likes in your Open Humans account, in an ongoing manner. Once you add this data to Open Humans, you can choose to share or explore it!

Check out this project's activity page to see how notebooks, projects, and members are using & sharing this data. (Once connected, you can also find your data on this page!)

This site is managed by Open Humans and follows the same terms of use. Data stored in Open Humans is private by default: it's your personal archive and always available to you, and you choose when to share with others.
