Data access & data management

Twitter data: The Twitter API gives this project ongoing access to data, which we will collect and store for you in Open Humans in an ongoing manner. This includes: your tweets with all their metadata (e.g. links to images, geolocation if provided), your liked tweets with all their metadata, your follower & following counts.

Data files created by the app are stored in your Open Humans account and are always visible to you on the project activity page.

The Twitter API also gives us access to data we will not copy into your Open Humans account, including: username, lists of your followers/followings, and other activities on their site. Access to this data is unavoidable due to how Twitter operates, but we won't store it.

Other data: This project stores tokens for Open Humans and Twitter authorizations, but has no further access to your Open Humans account data beyond the data files it stores.

Removing this connection: You can visit the project activity page in Open Humans to remove this connection within Open Humans and, optionally, delete associated data files.
